Places of Interest Nearby


  1. El Pavón Waterfall: This stunning waterfall is just 10 minutes walking down the road from this property. It is one of the most known waterfalls in the country. And it is considered one of the most beautiful around the country due to its imposing view. And it is just as far as talking a walk in the afternoon and bathing in its pristine waters. Besides, its location is immesurably important as it receives hundreds of visitors each year from countries abroad. Making of it a paradaisical destination perfect for a renting villa or small lodge.
  2. Marino Ballena National Park: This national park is known for its stunning beaches and the famous Whale's Tail, a sandbar formation that resembles a whale's tail during low tide. You can enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and whale watching in this beautiful coastal area.
  3. Corcovado National Park: Considered one of the most biodiverse places on Earth, Corcovado National Park offers incredible rainforest experiences. Hike through lush trails, spot various wildlife species including monkeys, sloths, and jaguars, and immerse yourself in the pristine natural surroundings.
  4. Nauyaca Waterfalls: Located just a short distance from Vergel, Nauyaca Waterfalls is a stunning natural attraction. You can hike to the waterfalls and swim in the crystal-clear pools while surrounded by lush jungle scenery.
  5. Bahia Ballena: This beautiful bay is known for its natural beauty and calm waters. You can relax on the sandy beach, go for a swim, or explore the mangrove forests by kayak or paddleboard.
  6. Ventanas Beach: Ventanas Beach is another beautiful beach close to Vergel. It is known for its unique arc with a double cave carved by the oceanic forces and offers great opportunities for swimming, sunbathing, and beach picnics. At just 15 minutes.
  7. Sierpe River: Take a boat tour along the Sierpe River and explore the mangroves, where you can spot wildlife like crocodiles, monkeys, and a variety of bird species. The river tour also leads to the stunning Sierpe Terraba Wetlands, which are a Ramsar-protected site.
  8. Dominical: Enjoy visiting Dominical downtown, the most well know beach for surfers and adventures. Find yourself walking along the beach and mesmerizing yourself with the salty breeze that meets the prosperous leafy forests of the Pacific Coast. You might as well find a piece of paradise every time you visit the stores with bright colors. And wonderful restaurants with the finnest foods.
